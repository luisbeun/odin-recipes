Vanilla donut with sugar

Donuts are a common and sweet pastry that everyone loves, and more with that sweet white sugar on top

There are a lot of recipes but today we are going to make a simple vanilla donut

50 minutes 10 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of self-rising flour
  • 1 cups of sugar
  • 3eggs
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 cup of milk
  • Vanilla essence (to taste)

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar with the eggs
  2. Add the oil and mix well, then add the milk and vanilla essence.
  3. Gradually add the previously sifted flour.
  4. Then incorporate everything into a buttered/floured mold, and bake until the dough is clean when you poke it with a fork
  5. Once it is cold, throw impalpable sugar on top
